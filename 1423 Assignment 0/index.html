Learn How to Drive a Car! And Feast.

Follow these instructions:

  1. Fasten your seatbelts.
  2. Start the car's engine until it's slightly purring.
  3. Look around left and right then slowly release the clutch.
  4. Disengage the handbrake cause we want to move!
    • If you're on an incline, make sure you don't go downhill. That would not be fun.
    • Do this by holding on the brake simultaneously.
  5. Hold the clutch at biting point then slowly release.
  6. Accelerate and then VROOOOM.
This is how a revving car sounds like!
